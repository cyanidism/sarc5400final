The National Debt is 34 Trillion Dollars. This is where its interests go to every year.

*All numbers are in dollar amounts

Accrual Basis GAS Expense: Incurring Government Account Series (GAS) interests regardless of when paid

Savings Bonds: Securities issued by the government to fund for public expenditures

Amortized Discount: Amount of interest expense when bonds are issued at a discount of their face value

Cash Basis GAS Payments: Interest payments on Government Account Series (GAS) when they are actually paid

Accrued Interest Expense: the interest that has been incurred but not yet paid on the national debt

source: US Treasury
